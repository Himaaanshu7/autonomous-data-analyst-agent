# 🎬 CapCut Step-by-Step Guide — Noctua Ad (90 sec)

Follow this guide exactly. By the end you'll have a professional 90-second feature demo ad ready to export.

---

## STEP 1 — Download & Setup

1. Download **CapCut Desktop** from capcut.com (free, no watermark)
2. Open CapCut → click **"New Project"**
3. Set resolution: **1920 × 1080 (16:9)**
4. Set frame rate: **30 fps**
5. Name the project: `Noctua Ad`
6. Click **Create**

---

## STEP 2 — Record These 7 Screen Clips First

Open your Noctua app at **http://localhost:8501** and record the following clips using Windows built-in recorder (`Win + G`). Load sample data first.

| Clip # | What to Record | Duration |
|--------|---------------|----------|
| Clip 1 | Type *"Show me top 5 customers by revenue"* → watch table appear | ~10s |
| Clip 2 | Ask *"Show revenue by category"* → bar chart appears. Then ask *"Show sales trend"* → line chart. Then *"Show correlation"* → heatmap. Fast actions. | ~15s |
| Clip 3 | Click **"Run Anomaly Scan"** in sidebar → watch scan run → anomaly chart with red dots appears | ~10s |
| Clip 4 | Type *"Forecast revenue for next 6 months"* → forecast chart with dashed line and shaded band appears | ~10s |
| Clip 5 | Type *"Show cohort retention"* → heatmap appears. Then *"Create a pivot table of sales by region"* → pivot table appears | ~10s |
| Clip 6 | Click the **KPI Dashboard** tab → scroll through metric cards and charts slowly | ~8s |
| Clip 7 | In Settings → click **Export Chat PDF** → download button appears → open the PDF showing Noctua branding | ~8s |

> **Tip:** Do a practice run first. Then record the real take. Slow, deliberate mouse movements look better on video.

## STEP 3 — Add AI Voiceover

1. In CapCut, go to **Audio → Text to Speech (AI Voice)**
2. Paste this exact script:

```
What if you could hire a data analyst that never sleeps, never misses anything, and answers in seconds?

Most businesses are sitting on goldmines of data they never use. Because querying databases takes SQL. Spotting trends takes expertise. Building reports takes hours. And by the time you have answers — the moment has passed.

Meet Noctua. An autonomous AI data analyst that understands your data, thinks like an analyst, and delivers boardroom-ready insights — just from a single question.

No SQL. No formulas. Just ask. Type any question in plain English and Noctua writes the query, runs it, and returns the answer in seconds.

Every answer comes with the perfect chart — automatically. Bar charts, line graphs, scatter plots, heatmaps, histograms. Noctua picks the right one every time.

Noctua watches your data like a hawk. Run an autonomous scan and it flags every statistical outlier across all your tables — before they turn into real problems.

Ask Noctua to predict next quarter's revenue. It fits a trend model, projects forward, and shows you confidence intervals — just like a professional financial analyst.

Cohort retention. Statistical significance tests. Pivot tables. Data cleaning. Validation checks. Noctua handles analyses that used to take your team days — in under a minute.

Your live KPI dashboard shows real-time metrics the moment you load your data. Export any report as a polished PDF or Excel file in one click.

Noctua. See everything in your data. Try it free today.
```

3. Select voice: **"Calm Male"** or **"Professional Male"** (deep, authoritative tone)
4. Set speed to **0.95x** (slightly slower = more gravitas)
5. Click **Generate** → drag the audio onto your timeline

## STEP 4 — Build the Timeline (Shot by Shot)

Add clips to the timeline in this exact order. Match each clip to its timestamp.

| Timestamp | What to Add | How |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| **0 – 6s** | Black background + 🦉 emoji centered + text: *"What if your data could just talk to you?"* | Add solid black background. Add text overlay. |
| **6 – 18s** | Search CapCut stock footage: *"stressed office work spreadsheet"* → pick best result | Drag stock clip to timeline, trim to 12s |
| **18 – 28s** | Black background + NOCTUA logo text + tagline *"AI Data Analyst"* | Text overlay, animated fade in |
| **28 – 38s** | **Clip 1** — natural language query demo | Drag your screen recording |
| **38 – 48s** | **Clip 2** — charts appearing (cut between bar/line/heatmap every 3-4s) | Drag and cut at 3-4s intervals |
| **48 – 58s** | **Clip 3** — anomaly scan running | Drag your screen recording |
| **58 – 68s** | **Clip 4** — forecast chart | Drag your screen recording |
| **68 – 78s** | **Clip 5** — cohort + pivot (cut halfway) | Drag, cut at 5s mark |
| **78 – 86s** | **Clip 6** (KPI dashboard, 4s) + **Clip 7** (PDF export, 4s) | Two clips back to back |
| **86 – 90s** | Black background + NOCTUA + *"See everything in your data"* + URL | Text overlays, fade in |

## STEP 6 — Colors & Font Settings

Apply these settings consistently across all text:

- **Background color:** `#0f2027` (deep navy — use for solid background clips)
- **Primary text:** White `#ffffff`
- **Accent text:** Teal `#a8edea`
- **Alert text:** Red `#ff4d4d` (anomaly section only)
- **Font:** Montserrat Bold (search in CapCut fonts)
- **Letter spacing:** +3 on all headlines
- **Headlines:** ALL CAPS

---

## STEP 7 — Background Music

1. Go to **Audio → Music → Search**
2. Search: `"Dark Cinematic Tech"`
3. Pick any track under **Cinematic → Corporate Tech** category
4. Drag it under your full timeline (0 – 90s)
5. Set music volume to **30%** (voiceover must stay dominant)
6. At **88s**, right-click the music clip → **Add Fade Out** (2s fade)

---

## STEP 8 — Transitions

Add these between clips only — keep it minimal:

| Between | Transition | Duration |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Every feature clip | **Fade** | 0.3s |
| Before CTA (86s) | **Zoom Out** | 0.5s |
| Hook → Problem | **Fade to Black** | 0.5s |

To add: click the **small square icon** between two clips on the timeline → select transition.

---

## STEP 9 — Final Check Before Export

Go through this checklist:

- [ ] Voiceover audio is clear and not cut off
- [ ] Music volume is lower than voiceover
- [ ] All 7 screen recording clips are in the right order
- [ ] Text overlays appear at the right timestamps
- [ ] Teal color `#a8edea` used consistently for accents
- [ ] CTA shows the correct URL
- [ ] Music fades out at the end
- [ ] Total duration is ~90 seconds

---

## STEP 10 — Export

1. Click **Export** (top right)
2. Set resolution: **1080p**
3. Set format: **MP4**
4. Set frame rate: **30 fps**
5. Click **Export**
6. Save to Desktop as `noctua_ad.mp4`
